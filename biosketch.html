Short Introduction

Prof. K. K. Pant is currently the Petrotech (FIPI) Chair Professor and Head of Chemical Engineering Department at IIT Delhi. He also serves as Adjunct faculty at University of Saskatchewan in Canada, Joint Faculty at CRDT, IIT Delhi and Honorary Faculty at University of Queensland in Australia. He is among the leading academicians in India who is working on cutting edge and futuristic technologies of national and international importance like coal to methanol conversion, e-waste and plastic management, hydrogen generation, CO2 capture and conversion, biomass valorization, etc., using catalysis and reaction engineering. Prof. Pant academic and industrial research experience spans over 30 years with 150+ publications having 8300 citations and numerous patents. He has successfully completed over 50 high impact projects and consultancies valued more than 500 million Indian Rupees from premier companies and government organizations.

Prof. Pant has also been conferred twice with the prestigious Gandhian Young Technological Innovation (GYTI) award, besides several other honors such as CHEMCON distinguished speaker award, Herdilia Award by Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers, thrice with Dr. A V Rama Rao award for best Ph.D. supervision, Dr. S. S. Deshpande Award, etc.
