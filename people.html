Dr. Ashish Bohre

Dr. Ashish Bohre is a Ramalingaswami fellow at the Department of Chemical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Delhi. Prior to his association with IIT, he served as a Research Assistant Professor in the Department of Catalysis and Chemical Reaction Engineering, National Institute of Chemistry, Slovenia from 2017-2020. He received his Ph.D. degree in Chemistry from Dr. Harisingh Gour University, Sagar under the supervision of Prof. O. P. Shrivastava. Then, he carried out postdoctoral research with Dr. Basudeb Saha and Prof. R. K. Sharma at the Department of Chemistry, University of Delhi, India from 2013 to 2016. His current research is focused on the development of new generations of catalysts and computational models for the chemical transformation of lignocellulosic biomass and plastic waste materials to high-value chemicals and fuels.
